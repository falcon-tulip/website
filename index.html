What We Do

Educate young girls
about the art of puberty.

Millions of girls across the world face a lack of education when it comes to puberty and periods. There is also a huge stigma around these extremely natural and normal topics.

What is the Tulip Project?

Young leaders start here

The Tulip Project is started to guide the journey of young girls in exploring the developments of their bodies and asssisting them in the adventure of maturity.

Learn More

The reason I pursued Tulip is because menstrual inequity is something that I've always been exposed to through stories from my mother who grew up in India. I believe that girls should only be empowered by their natural needs, and it's my mission to make that a reality across the world.

Ujjaini Das, 2021
